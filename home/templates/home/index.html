How it works?

There are a number of ways to use Toll.ie:

  • Infrequent users of toll roads in Ireland can use toll.ie can use the pay-a-toll (or pay-a-penalty) option to pay one or more tolls. The pay-a-toll option can also be used to pay forfuture journeys.
  • Frequent Users of the toll roads in Ireland can sign up to Toll.ie, register their Personal, Vehicle, and Payment details. This allows Toll.ie to pay for tolls as they are made, or charge for tolls on a monthly basis in arrears.
  • Toll.ie plans to privide a prepaid service in the future which would allow for credit to be associated with the user's vehicle(s), and charges would be taken from this. Keep an eye on Toll.ie, for further information.
